Our vision

Galion’s ambition is second to none.
We aim to help people navigate the rapidly changing financial world as its being reinvented by blockchain entrepreneurs.

Provide a clear picture of your financial situation and the market.

Galion will allow you to easily track an ever growing number of crypto assets, on an ever increasing number of accounts (exchanges accounts, wallet addresses, third party services) alongside your traditional financial assets (including traditional exchanges and bank accounts).

Empower you to make smarter decisions for your money.

Galion will allow you to make informed decisions, by providing in-depth analysis of your portfolio performances, expenses and revenues in a clear interface and point you to the next best move for your particular situation.

Enable access to all your financial services in one place.

Galion will allow you to easily access the third party financial services emerging in the crypto ecosystem as well as legacy ones (credit cards, loans etc.). You will not have to use ten apps to manage your personal finances anymore, making more efficient use of your time.

Our MVP

This project was born of the lack of a good portfolio management solution, which the market needed following the quick rise in the number of blockchains and tokens. To address this we developed our MVP, which is already the best portfolio tracker on the market, and will quickly turn into so much more as we progress towards our vision.

Clear financial picture Automated tracking Ledger integration

Clear financial picture
Quickly check the evolution of your portfolio, and what you currently own without having to manually input every transaction you make.
Use our entirely modular approach based on accounts, portfolios and overall picture to tailor your portfolio tracking experience to your personal needs.
Stay up to date on the market by visualizing the Coinmarketcap top 100.
The Galion MVP is fully responsive, so you can use it via your mobile or tablet. Stay tuned, mobile apps are the first major milestones of our dense roadmap!

Automated tracking
Galion is interfaced with exchanges APIs and blockchains. The value of every compatible account you have is automatically synced with Galion, without the need for any human intervention. Currently supported blockchains and accounts: Bitcoin, Ethereum, Bitcoin Cash, Binance, Kraken, Bittrex. More coming soon!
Of course, if you wish to track your investments on accounts that are not yet supported by Galion, you can still manually add your transactions.

Ledger Integration
Use the Galion web app to generate and broadcast Ethereum transactions with your Ledger Nano S.
Since our vision is to make Galion much more than a tracking app, we wanted our users to feel that the first steps towards this vision are already in place within our MVP. We decided to integrate Ledger, because of its great track record and acclaimed security.

The Galion Token

  • For the Token Sale, we will issue the Galion Token, which will be a standard ERC-20 token, running natively on the Ethereum Blockchain. A maximum of 320M GLN will be issued during the token sale.
  • This token will be used to pay for the fees of the premium features Galion will offer soon, with a significant discount of 50%.
  • Part of the tokens we will received as payment will be burnt, thus decreasing the supply as time goes by. An announcement and proof will be provided each time a burning operation takes place.

Galion’s token sale

  • We are proud to say we have gone to great lengths to guarantee a fair and accessible token sale for all participants.

The pre-sale

  • A maximum of 33% of our hard cap will be allocated to presale participants. The pre-sale will close at least two weeks before the start of the main sale. If you wish to contribute to the pre-sale, please email us at presale@galion.io

The main sale

  • The main sale will only be open to whitelisted individuals, and will be operated through a smart contract on the Ethereum blockchain, which has been audited by a third party. Only participations in Ether will be accepted for the main sale.
  • The whitelist is now open, and will close a few days before the start of the main sale.
  • To ensure a fair repartition and a stress-free sale for everyone, we will implement a 12 hours individual cap: after 12 hours you will be able to contribute any amount you wish.
  • The smart contract address will be announced at least one day before the start of the main sale only on the website. Please be sure to check that you are on the Galion.io website and that you have a correct certificate in your navigator before any transaction with the smart contract.
  • The main sale will run for 3 weeks or until the hard cap is reached. You will be notified by email the day prior and an hour before the smart contract starts accepting contributions.

Fund use

  • The funds raised during our token sale will be spent as follows:
  • Considering our very dense product roadmap, we expect that at least 40% of our funds will be spent in product development.
  • Since the viability of our project strongly depends on the number of users we have, 40% of our funds will be spent in marketing & customer acquisition.
  • The 20% of funds remaining will be kept in reserve, to allow the company to face unexpected situations.

Peace of mind for participants: legal and security considerations.

Legal and security considerations are a major issue for token sales. Providing our community with the best possible participation conditions is of paramount importance to us:

  • We have partnered with Fieldfisher and their token sale specialized team to ensure full compliance with the law. We presented our project to the French equivalent of the SEC, the AMF, in order to get ahead of future requirements and ensure that our procedures are above and beyond the standards.
  • We also partnered with LegalUp Consulting, a law firm specialized in data protection and privacy law to ensure we are fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation, a European Union framework and the leading consumer privacy protection to date.
  • We run Galion using the most up-to-date and effective security procedures, including employee training, stringent security policy including mandatory two factor authentication across the board and full disk encryption, among others.
  • We have taken significant steps to ensure resistance to DDoS, personal data leaks, and chose the most secure certificate with extended verification to authenticate our website.
  • The token sale smart contract has been thoroughly audited, including by a third party.
