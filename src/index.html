Our vision

Get a clear picture of your financial situation and the market.

Follow all your cryptocurrencies and traditional accounts in one place whether they are exchanges accounts, blockchain addresses or bank accounts.

Make smarter decisions for your money

Make informed decision, by providing in-depth analysis of your portfolio performances, expenses and revenues and point you to the next best move for your particular situation.

Galion will integrate all the financial services you need to manage your assets. You will never feel the need to use another app.

Galion key features

The Galion website already automatically handles ethereum and bitcoin wallets, as well as Kraken, Bittrex, Bitfinex, and liqui.io accounts.

We add new supported exchanges every week. Simply log in, enter your information, and see how much you have, spent, and transferred, with a complete historic view.

Galion is a french company operating out of Paris, which comes with many benefits for our users: the peace of mind of a well regulated environment, be it security-wise or privacy-wise. As a Galion user, your data is protected by the European General Data Protection Regulation.
